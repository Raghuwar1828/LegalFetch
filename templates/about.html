About Legal Fetch

Legal Fetch is a tool designed to automatically extract and summarize the Terms of Service (TOS) and Privacy Policy (PP) of websites.

By scraping these documents, we aim to help users quickly understand a website's terms without needing to read lengthy legal text.

How It Works:

  • Input one or more website domains (e.g., example.com).
  • Select which document to analyze: Terms of Service or Privacy Policy.
  • Our system automatically finds and extracts the correct legal document.
  • AI-powered summarization creates both detailed (100-word) and brief (25-word) summaries.
  • Advanced text analysis provides readability scores, sentiment analysis, and key term identification.
  • Results are cached for faster retrieval in future searches.

Key Features:

  • Cloudflare & CAPTCHA Detection - Automatically identifies and reports websites protected by security measures.
  • Text Mining Metrics - Calculates readability scores, sentiment analysis, and identifies statistically significant terms.
  • Filter by Agreement Type - Focus on either Terms of Service or Privacy Policy documents.
  • SQL Viewer - Authenticated users can explore the database with filtering, column selection, and auto-refresh capabilities.
  • API Access - Programmatically access summary data through our REST API.

Why It Matters:

Understanding the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy of websites is essential for protecting user privacy and ensuring compliance with various legal agreements.

This tool helps you save time and make informed decisions by offering easy-to-understand summaries and key insights through natural language processing.

Technical Implementation:

Legal Fetch is built with Python and Flask, utilizing advanced NLP techniques and the Gemini AI API for summarization. The system features robust error handling, caching mechanisms, and security features to ensure reliable operation.
